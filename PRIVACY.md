# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**OneLife - Make Time Count**

Last updated: June 11, 2026

## English

OneLife does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- Everything you enter (date of birth, life expectancy, daily journal entries, theme settings, background photos) is stored **only on your device** (and synced to your Apple Watch via Apple's local device-to-device channel, where applicable).
- The app contains **no analytics, no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no user accounts**, and it makes **no network requests**.
- Photos you choose as backgrounds or poster images are read only on-device and are never uploaded anywhere.
- Posters you save are written to your photo library only at your explicit request.
- Local notifications (bedtime reminders) are scheduled entirely on your device.
- Deleting the app deletes all of its data.

If you have any questions, contact: rjxznb1@gmail.com

## 中文

OneLife 不收集、不存储、不传输、不共享任何个人数据。

- 你输入的一切内容（出生日期、预期寿命、每日打卡记录、主题设置、背景照片）**仅保存在你的设备本地**（如使用 Apple Watch，则通过 Apple 的本地设备间通道同步到手表）。
- 本应用**没有数据统计、没有广告、没有任何追踪 SDK、没有用户账号体系**，也**不发起任何网络请求**。
- 你选择作为背景或海报的照片仅在设备上读取，绝不会被上传。
- 保存的海报仅在你主动操作时写入系统相册。
- 本地通知（睡前提醒）完全在设备上调度。
- 删除应用即删除其全部数据。

如有任何疑问，请联系：rjxznb1@gmail.com
